You give your staff, members, and club your all every day. But as the saying goes, you can’t pour from an empty cup. Join your industry for five days of professional development and peer networking (plus a little fun along the way) to replenish your reserves and keep giving them everything you’ve got—and then some. CMAA’s World Conference and Club Business Expo covers it all, giving you everything you need to enhance your skills and stay competitive in an ever-evolving industry, in addition to robust educational programming with sessions on hiring, marketing, food and beverage, finance, member relations and more.

Travel

Hotel

CMAA has negotiated rates at several hotels near the Music City Center ranging in price from $224-$249 nightly plus taxes and fees (currently 15.45% plus a $2.50 assessment charge per room per night.) Due to significantly increasing demand, CMAA is actively working with ConferenceDirect and hotels in Nashville to add availability to our room block. *Subject to change

ConferenceDirect is the official housing provider for the CMAA 2019 World Conference and Club Business Expo. For your own protection, please do not make reservations through any unknown housing or travel company. If you receive a phone call or email from any company that is not our official housing provider, ConferenceDirect, please contact CMAA immediately at conference@cmaa.org.
