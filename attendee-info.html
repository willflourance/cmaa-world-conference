Registration Instructions

REGISTRATION CATEGORIES (PDF)

Select Individual Registration on the main event page – you will be prompted to login.

CMAA Members

  • On the page “Sign In or Activate Credentials.”
  • If you have current login credentials (you have logged in using the new format as of June 2018.)
  • Login, then proceed with your registration.

Non-Members

  • On the page “Sign In or Activate Credentials.”
  • Create or Update your login credentials.
  • Beneath “Not a registered user yet?” select Register.
  • Provide your Email, Name and a DOB, then click Search.
  • Follow the prompts to create a new account or use the red Forgot Password button to complete the login process.

Once you have successfully logged in

  • In the Registrant Type dropdown, select Attendee.
  • Select your preference beneath Event Fees. You should only choose ONE Event Fees option.
  • Please note the Full Registration categories include a ticket to the Networking Event; you will need to purchase a ticket separately in any other category if desired.
  • If adding a Significant Other, please use the Add Guest button near the bottom of the page.
  • Select your preference beneath Sessions.

Adding Significant Other Registrations

  • Please use the Add Guest button near the bottom of the page if adding at the same time.
  • If you are adding a significant other registration at a later date, after logging in, select My Events from the right-side menu. Select your existing registration, then use the Add Guest button.

What is the Cancellation Policy?

Cancellation of full registration categories will be accepted until January 24, 2019, with no penalty. A refund of the full conference fee – minus a 25 percent penalty – will be given for cancellations received by February 22, 2019. No refunds will be granted for requests received after February 22, 2019. All registration cancellations and refund requests must be made in writing by the individual whose name appears on the registration. To be valid, submit all requests to CMAA Registration at cmaa.org/cancelevent.aspx. CMAA reserves the right to assess a $50 penalty fee if requests are made to change to a lesser registration category. No refunds will be issued for the following registration categories: Business Access or Faculty, at any time. CMAA regrets that refunds will not be given for no-shows. Substitutions are gladly accepted; substitutions of registrations are permitted prior to the Conference and on site. Only one substitution is permitted per original registrant. The individual submitting the substitution request is responsible for all financial obligations (any balance due) associated with the substitution. Badge sharing, splitting, and reprints are strictly prohibited.

What is the Dress Code for Conference?

  • All Education and the Opening and Closing Business Sessions will be business casual.
  • The Networking Event at the Ryman Auditorium will be Nashville casual and boots are highly encouraged.

Where will Events be held in Nashville?

All education and business sessions, the Club Business Expo, registration, and most Conference events (excluding the Networking Event) will take place at the Music City Center, 201 5th Avenue S, Nashville, TN. Rooms blocks have been established at three conveniently located hotels.

I’m a First-Time Attendee. How do I get the most out of the Event?

An online orientation webinar will be held in January for those attending the World Conference for the first time. Watch your email for an invitation to participate! On-site, you can participate in the First-Time Attendee Gathering on Sunday, February 24, at 10:45 a.m. and connect with other first-time attendees.

How do I earn the most available Credits at Conference?

  • By attending all scheduled sessions of education, you can earn up to 22.5 Club Management Institute (CMI) Education Credits.
  • Complete your session evaluation survey correctly on the CMAA Events App. Please enter the attendance code provided at each session.
  • Extend your trip and add a Pre-Conference Workshop and earn six CMI credits.
  • Register for a Full Member Registration. This registration category earns 12 Association Activity Credits, the most of any category.

Do you have a resource to help me justify my attendance to my Board/Management team?

We know that attending the CMAA World Conference and Club Business Expo is an investment of your time and your club’s financial resources. CMAA has created tools and resources, available here, to assist you in making attendance a win-win for you and your club!

How will Conference information be communicated?

  • All advance communications including confirmations and reminders will be distributed via email. Make sure the address cmaa.org is on your email safe senders list. If you have not been receiving current announcements from CMAA or if you have opted out of any emails in the past, please visit the Get Mail page at cmaa.org/getmail.aspx to make sure CMAA has your current information.
  • Communications, including on-site schedule changes, will be communicated via the Conference Events App. Make your in-App notifications are turned on for the latest breaking news.
  • Daily news will be distributed each morning via email through the Conference Daily.

Does CMAA provide travel accommodations for the World Conference?

ConferenceDirect is the official housing provider for the CMAA 2019 World Conference and Club Business Expo. For your own protection, please do not make reservations through any unknown housing or travel company. If you receive a phone call or email from any company that is not our official housing provider, ConferenceDirect, please contact CMAA immediately at conference@cmaa.org.

CMAA makes every effort to keep attendee expenses at the World Conference including registration fees and accommodation rates for the event as low as possible. We work diligently to negotiate the best hotel rates and to make the best use of your registration dollars to keep the Conference affordable. CMAA encourages all participants to reserve a room with ConferenceDirect at one of the CMAA host hotels; this supports not only the 2019 World Conference, but also future meetings.

Housing Reservations: Online at https://book.passkey.com/go/2019CMAA or over the phone at 844-572-7253. All housing related questions can be directed to CMAA@conferencedirect.com.

Deadline: 5:00 p.m. CST, Friday, February 1 or as availability allows

 *Please note: The Club Business Expo and all education sessions will take place at the Music City Center.

Due to the increasing demand for rooms in the CMAA room block and in a continued effort to keep attendee expenses low, the hotels listed above will require a non-refundable deposit of one night’s room and tax, to be charge to the card on file on February 2. Please note for any reservations cancelled in advance of this date there will be a $30 per reservation cancellation processing charge. This is a one-time fee for any cancelled reservation regardless of the time period in which they are cancelled.

My preferred dates are not available. Can I go on a waitlist?

Select the waitlist option when available on the booking website or contact ConferenceDirect at 844-572-7253 to inquire about waitlist availability. Please note that there are currently several dates where the hotels are sold out and, in that occurrence, a waitlist will not be available.

I still do not see my preferred housing dates. What should I do?

CMAA is actively working with ConferenceDirect and hotels in Nashville to add availability to our room block. However, CMAA is competing with several other meetings and conferences in Nashville at the same time. Please refer back to the booking website as it is updated in real time and will have the most accurate availability. In the meantime, you are encouraged to book at the hotel showing the largest majority of your desired stay and contact ConferenceDirect to modify your reservation should you need to add days or move hotels. Please note that availability changes often and CMAA cannot guarantee future availability.


We’d love to see you in Nashville and understand you may opt to book one of the many hotels in proximity to the Music City Center. Nashville is a small city that is easy to get around, so access to CMAA’s World Conference should still be convenient.
